Who We Are

Clean Sweep (also known as Seva Setu) is a comprehensive civic-tech initiative designed to empower communities to take charge of their environment and society. We go beyond just clean-up drives. Our platform bridges the gap between eager volunteers and dedicated organizers such as Panchayats, NGOs, and student groups across a wide spectrum of social causes.

Environmental Stewardship

From tree plantations to waste management, we protect our planet.

Health & Wellness

Organizing blood donation camps and health checkups for the underserved.

Education Support

Tutoring, book drives, and digital literacy workshops.

Disaster Relief

Quick mobilization of volunteers during local emergencies.

Our Vision 2027

  • Mobilize 1 Million+ volunteers across India.
  • facilitate 50,000+ community events annually.
  • Create a self-sustaining ecosystem of civic responsibility.
